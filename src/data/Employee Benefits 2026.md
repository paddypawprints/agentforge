# **Employee Benefits 2026**

## **1\. Health and Wellness**

Employees are provided with comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance. Coverage begins on the first day of employment.

### **1.1 Sustainable Commuting**

To support environmental goals and physical health, the company provides a bicycle maintenance stipend. Employees are eligible for a $150.00 annual reimbursement for repairs, tune-ups, and safety equipment related to bicycle commuting.

## **2\. Remote Work Support**

Employees working in a remote or hybrid capacity are eligible for equipment reimbursements to ensure a safe and ergonomic workspace.

### **2.1 Ergonomic Equipment**

The company provides a one-time reimbursement for the purchase of an ergonomic standing desk. The maximum reimbursement limit is $250.00. A valid receipt must be submitted through the internal expense portal within 30 days of purchase.

### **2.2 Technology Allowance**

A monthly stipend of $50.00 is provided to cover high-speed internet costs for remote employees.

## **3\. Professional Development**

Continuous learning is a core value. We provide resources for employees to expand their technical and leadership skills.

### **3.1 Training Budget**

Each employee is allocated a professional development budget of $2,500.00 per calendar year. This budget can be used for:

* Industry-recognized certifications  
* Technical workshops and bootcamps  
* Academic courses relevant to the current role  
* Professional conference attendance and travel

Approval from a direct manager is required before registering for any program exceeding $500.00.

## **4\. Leave Policies**

Full-time employees receive 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per year, accrued semi-monthly. In addition, the company observes 12 public holidays and provides 10 days of dedicated sick leave.

## **5\. Retirement and Savings**

The company offers a 401(k) program with a matching contribution of 100% on the first 4% of an employee's salary. Matching funds are subject to a 2-year vesting schedule.
